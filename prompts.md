## Prompt 5: Interactive Website Design (V0)
**Live Demo:** https://v0-interactive-website-design-mu.vercel.app/

> The website background is a deep reddish-black, creating a dark and immersive atmosphere.
>
> In the center of the layout, there are small glowing dots placed at regular intervals. These dots are connected by a single curving line that moves like a snake, flowing smoothly through them. This curvy line runs across the entire website, subtly animating in the background and visually linking all sections.
>
> On the right side of the page, there are vertically stacked boxes that move from bottom to top as the user scrolls. These scroll-based animations give the page a dynamic, living feel.
>
> On the left side, there are separate rounded-rectangle boxes labeled Power, Story, and Fracture. On the right side, there are similar rounded-rectangle boxes labeled Connection, Reality, and Upside-Down.
>
> All boxes follow a black-and-red theme and are slightly darker than the background to create depth. The edges of the boxes are softly rounded, and they appear elevated through subtle shadows or glow effects.
>
> The entire website is interactive: scrolling triggers smooth animations, background elements flow continuously, and all motion feels synchronized and intentional, reinforcing the theme of fracture, connection, and reality.
